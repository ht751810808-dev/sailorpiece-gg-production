Devil Fruits in Sailor Piece — Complete Overview

Devil Fruits are the most powerful progression system in Sailor Piece. Each fruit grants the user a unique set of abilities that define their entire combat playstyle — from the fire-breathing Dragon Fruit to the gravity-pulling Dark Fruit. In the game's lore (inspired by the One Piece anime), eating a Devil Fruit grants extraordinary powers at the cost of losing the ability to swim, which in a sea-faring game makes every fruit a high-stakes choice.

There are currently 14 Devil Fruits documented in Sailor Piece, spanning five rarity tiers from Common to Mythical. Each fruit has a unique damage type, a set of unlockable abilities tied to mastery levels, and a placement in the community tier list. The tier system runs from S+ (best in game) down to D (not recommended).

Fruits are obtained through spinning at the Fruit Dealer NPC, defeating certain Bosses, or trading with other players. Since each player can only use one fruit at a time, choosing the right fruit for your playstyle — PvP, PvE farming, or boss grinding — is one of the most important decisions in the game. This database covers every fruit with full stats, ability lists, pros and cons, and a tier ranking so you can make an informed choice.

Fruit Types Explained: Logia, Paramecia, and Zoan

Logia (Elemental)

Logia fruits grant the user the ability to transform their body into a natural element — fire, light, snow, magma, darkness, or ice. The defining trait is elemental immunity: at low mastery, physical attacks pass right through the user's body. This makes Logia fruits highly forgiving for beginners and strong in mid-game PvP. In Sailor Piece, Logia fruits are classified as "Elemental" type. Current Logia fruits: Light, Snow, Magma, Flame, Ice, Dark.

Paramecia (Natural)

Paramecia fruits grant special body modifications or environmental power — controlling shockwaves, generating explosions, manipulating blood, turning invisible, or spinning. They do not grant elemental immunity, but they often have higher raw damage output and unique mechanics. The strongest fruits in the game — Fiend Fruit's life-steal and Quake Fruit's AoE shockwaves — are both Paramecia. In Sailor Piece, classified as "Natural" type. Current Natural fruits: Fiend, Quake, Spirit, Bomb, Dark, Invisible, Spin.

Zoan (Beast)

Zoan fruits allow the user to transform into a powerful creature or beast. The transformation grants a large bonus to HP, access to beast-form abilities, and often flight. In Sailor Piece, the two current Zoan fruits are Dragon Fruit (transforms into an Eastern Dragon) and Kitsune Fruit (fox spirit form). Zoan fruits are classified as "Beast" type. Both are Mythical rarity, making them the hardest to obtain but among the strongest in the game. The Dragon transformation provides flight and high burst damage, while the Kitsune form gives a Lucky multiplier for rare farming.

How to Get Devil Fruits in Sailor Piece

Fruit Dealer Spin

Recommended

The primary method. Visit the Fruit Dealer NPC and spend 50 Gems or 15,000 Cash for one spin. Each spin randomly assigns a fruit based on rarity probabilities: Common 49.5%, Rare 30%, Epic 15%, Legendary 5%, Mythical 0.5%. You can spin as many times as resources allow.

Boss Drops

High-Level

Certain Bosses in Sailor Piece drop Devil Fruits upon defeat. This is the main alternative path to obtaining Mythical fruits without spinning. Boss fruit drops are rare, but fighting bosses also rewards other valuable items, making it efficient to farm even without a guaranteed drop.

Player Trading

Social

Sailor Piece supports a player trading system where you can exchange fruits with other players. If you already have a Mythical fruit you do not want, you can trade it for another specific Mythical or a bundle of lower-rarity fruits. Note: items from some CCU codes are marked as untradeable.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you get Devil Fruits in Sailor Piece?

The primary method is spinning at the Fruit Dealer NPC for 50 Gems or 15,000 Cash. Fruits can also drop from Bosses, making boss farming a reliable path to Mythical fruits. A player trading system also allows you to exchange fruits with others.

What are the rarest fruits?

Mythical-rarity fruits — Dragon Fruit, Fiend Fruit, and Kitsune Fruit — are the rarest at 0.5% per spin. They are also the strongest fruits in the game, rating S+ in the tier list.

Can you have two Devil Fruits at once?

No. Like in the One Piece universe, each player can only equip one Devil Fruit at a time. Choose carefully and invest mastery points into your chosen fruit.

What does mastery do for fruits?

Mastery unlocks additional abilities for your fruit. Most fruits start with one ability at mastery 1 and unlock further skills at mastery 50, 75, or 100. Flight abilities for Logia fruits (like Flame and Light) are typically locked behind mastery milestones.
